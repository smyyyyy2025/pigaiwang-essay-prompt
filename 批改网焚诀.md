# 七、万能高分模板框架

可直接套用，填入主题词即可：

```text
First and foremost, it is universally acknowledged that [核心观点]. Indeed, there is no denying that [进一步阐释]. In other words, when it comes to [主题词], [主题词] serves as the cornerstone of our approach. Needless to say, nothing can rival the significance of [主题词].

Furthermore, it goes without saying that [第二点论点]. Specifically, [具体描述]. For instance, [举例说明]. In a nutshell, nothing can rival the value of [主题词] in this regard. Indeed, this is of paramount importance for [受益对象]. On no account can we afford to overlook this point.

As a result, [结论/后果]. What's more, we must spare no effort to [具体措施]. Frankly speaking, we must make the most of [主题词] by [方式方法]. Ultimately, nothing but [主题词] can ensure our continued success. Only in this way can we [展望未来].
```

# 八、避坑规则
- 只看红色和橙色，绿色学习提示一律忽略

- 不用连字符复合形容词（如 people-centered 改 centered on the people）

- political position/commitment/principle 容易报“疑似中式英语”，优先用 political standpoint / political tenet / political character

- founding spirit 容易报“疑似中式英语”，改用 the very spirit 或 the great spirit

- 关键主题词反复出现，不用代词 it/they/this 替代

- 每个句子开头必有连词，不惜堆到机械的程度

- 每句话必有闪光，宁可看起来笨重，也要让系统识别

- 见好就收，错误清零后不再大改
